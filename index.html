I am a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Bielefeld in Bielefeld, Germany. I am currently working in Prof. Philipp Cimiano's group, AG Semantic Computing. My research interests include the following:

  • Ontologies and the lexicon-ontology interface
  • Collaborative development and publishing of language resources
  • Big data and data science
  • Linked data and the Semantic Web
  • Machine translation and multilingualism
  • Machine learning methods for NLP
  • Probabilistic inference
